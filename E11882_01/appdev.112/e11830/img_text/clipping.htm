This is a text description of Figure 7-10, "Clipping, Splitting, and Concatenating Geometric Segments". It illustrates clipping (part a), splitting (part b), and concatenating (part c) a geometric segment. In this illustration, clipping results in a smaller segment that is a portion of the original segment, splitting breaks the segment into two segments, and concatenating forms one segment from two original segments. The direction of segments is preserved in all operations in the illustration.
