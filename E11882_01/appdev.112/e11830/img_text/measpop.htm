This is a text description of Figure 7-4, "Measure Populating of a Geometric Segment". It illustrates measure populating of a geometric segment, as described in text before and after the illustration. The assigned measure values (associated with small white circles) are proportional to their relative distances between the measure values that are not changed (associated with small shaded circles), but they are not proportional to relative distances across the entire geometric segment.
