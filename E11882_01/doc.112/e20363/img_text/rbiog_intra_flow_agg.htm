Shows the ORDM_AGG_DEP_FLW subprocess flow. This includes the Start_Process, a Fork, DWA_CUST_TYP_ORDR_SBC_WK_MV, DWA_PCHSE_ORDR_SBC_DAY_MV. These lead to either End_Warning or End_Error, or if all is good, DAW_CUST_TYP_ORDR_SBC_WK_MV goes on to DWA_CUST_TYPE_ODRD_DEPT_MO_MV flow and DWA_PCHSE_ORDR_SBC_DAY_MV goes on to DWA_PCHSE_ORDR_DEPT_MO_MV. Then there is an AND and then End_Success.
