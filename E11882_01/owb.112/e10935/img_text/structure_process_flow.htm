This image displays the Structure panel for a process flow. At the top of the panel are the following icons, from left to right: Freeze View, New View, New, and Delete. Below these icons are two nodes END_SUCCESS and NOTIFICATION. The NOTIFICATION node is expanded to display the following parameters: DEFAULT_RESPONSE, EXPAND_ROLES, HTML_BODY, PERFORMER, PRIORITY, RESPONSE_PROCESSOR, RESPONSE_TYPE, SUBJECT, TEXT_BODY, and TIMEOUT.
