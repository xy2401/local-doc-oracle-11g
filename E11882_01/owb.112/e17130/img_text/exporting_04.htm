The Export Advanced Options box contains sections Languages and Administration.

  • The Languages section lists the Base Language, and also provides two lists of languages, Available Languages and Selected Languages, with navigational icons for moving items between the two lists.

  • The Administration section contains checkboxes for Export user-defined definition and Export security information.

It also has the buttons Help, OK, and Cancel.
